About Me

I’m Majimbo Anyangu, an engineer with a strong foundation in both hardware and software. I hold a degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the Technical University of Kenya and am currently enhancing my software development skills through PLP Academy. My work sits at the intersection of engineering and AI, with a focus on dataset optimization and multilingual systems.

During my academic journey, I took part in a short exchange program at the National School of Engineers of Tarbes (ENIT), where I studied in French and gained deeper insight into integrated system design. This experience expanded both my technical and intercultural competence.

To support myself throughout my studies, I worked as a freelancer—teaching French and Swahili, and contributing to AI data projects. These roles sharpened my communication skills, deepened my understanding of real-world AI applications, and strengthened my adaptability.

I am passionate about building intelligent, practical solutions that drive impact. Whether working on AI systems, teaching, or engineering prototypes, I approach every project with curiosity, purpose, and a commitment to excellence.

Home Automation Using Bluetooth

The Home Automation Using Bluetooth project enables remote control of household appliances via a Bluetooth-enabled microcontroller. It uses an Android app to send control signals, processed by the microcontroller to switch devices on/off. Implemented using C++ (Arduino) for embedded programming, it integrates real-time communication protocols for seamless and responsive automation.

Demo Video Documentation

Soft-Active Robotic Hand

The Soft-Active Robotic Hand utilizes Nitinol shape-memory alloy (SMA) actuators for flexible and responsive motion. The system is microcontroller-driven, with Arduino (C++) programming controlling actuation. Sensors provide real-time feedback, enabling adaptive grasping. The Finite Element Method (FEM) is used for structural analysis, ensuring optimized force distribution. The design integrates embedded control algorithms for smooth, precise movements.
